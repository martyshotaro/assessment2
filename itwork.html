4th video - Day in a IT Technician Life by Eduvo Sdn Bhd

• What kind of work is done by the IT professional?

The kind of work that’s done by this IT professional includes receiving customer encounter problems such as complaints from the call centre (or Helpdesk) by creating a ticketing system for that specific customer. Also, they make appointments with the customer form their preferred date and time, and when the technician arrives, the technician starts troubleshooting. Afterwards, the technician tells the customer about the good news, then the customer will sign the confirmation paper (or a sign-off service report) afterwards, then to sum things up, the technician will close the ticket when the service is done.

• What kinds of people does the IT professional interact with? Are there other IT professionals? Clients? Investors? The general public?

In the video, he interacted with the customer for the most of the time and through the worker from the call centre (Helpdesk) via ticketing system due to a complaint of a customer.

• Where does the IT professional spend most of their time?

The IT professional spent most of his time troubleshooting the customer’s computer, as you can see in the video.

• What aspect of their position is most challenging?

The hardest aspect of being an IT technician is the troubleshooting aspect of IT. As you can see in the video, he opens the CPU to see what’s wrong, so that he can start troubleshooting. It didn’t work at first and it took him a bit of time to figure it out. However, he took a different approach of troubleshooting by replacing one of the CPU components for the customer’s computer to start running successfully. In general, sometimes troubleshooting can take a long time, or it can also stop working due to your profile and system being corrupted. In order for your computer to work, you must create a new user and check if the same issue occurs again.
